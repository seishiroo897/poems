Hi Kyle!

Another congratulations ulit!!! 🎉 Hindi man halata pero deep inside, I'm very happy for you and your achievements. You deserve every bit of success that comes your way, and honestly, even more. ✨

I'll be honest when I say that this day is bittersweet for me. Hindi na kita makikita after ng event kaya I'll give my everything for the last time.

I made this website just for you. Angas ba? HAHAHA Nandito yung mga best poems na ginawa ko, dedicated for you. Pwede mong i-click yung mga polaroid pictures and magpapakita na yung poem. Nahirapan ako do'n sa blooming flower animation sa previous mail na nakita mo, pero worth it naman HAHAHAHA.

Every poem here carries a piece of my heart. Some are whispers I was too shy to say aloud, others are dreams I've tucked between lines. When I couldn't find the right words to say, I wrote them down instead.

You've left something in me that I'll carry even after today, not in a heavy way, but in a way that makes me grateful. Grateful to have known you, grateful to have created something because of you.

Until we meet again (or not HAHAHA),
always rooting for you, always cheering for you 🎀
